--- title: "Rutherford Appleton Laboratory" excerpt: "Industrial placement
" collection: portfolio --- At the Rutherford Appleton Laboratory I was an industiral placement student for 6 months during my undergraduate. I was involved in the performance testing and redesign of mm-wave components for the Atacama Large Millimeter Array (ALMA) radio telescope. Specifically I was able to work on the local oscillator on Band 5, and was even able to contribute to a large meeting in Chile and visit the site in the Atacama desert.
